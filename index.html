A Little About Me

Software Engineer & Scrum Master for the Web.com Ads team. At Web, I'm contributing to a platform that helps small businesses find and build meaningful relationships with more of their target market.

I currently drive development on a system of 18 services powering the delivery and sync of millions of ads and bids to Google, Bing, and other marketing providers.

The bulk of my experience is in leveraging a cutting edge CI/CD pipeline based on the Mesosphere to serve clusterized microservices backed by MongoDB and Postgres, while utilizing in-house queuing libraries and job frameworks.

I also have experience with test driven development methodologies and testing frameworks such as Mockito, Selenium, Enzyme, Jest and JUnit.

I am a champion of agile/scrum in the organization, and work with product owners to ensure the engineers on my team enjoy a consistent mix of well defined product work and techdebt to push forward on.

As a part of the Scrum Master role I own the team's processes; Including sprint planning, standup, grooming, retro, and the end of sprint demo to the engineering, product, and executive organizations.
